Hailee Steinfeld Live in Manila


Hailee Steinfeld went here in Manila for her 3 days show. It is not a concert, just a mall show and album signing. I went on April 9, 2016 at Uptown Mall, BGC. Hailee is the star on Pitch Perfect 2 where she sang Jessy J 's flashlight. I love her because her voice is amazing. I will never forget that day because I was able to talk to her. Talk? Ah more like, cry in front of her! During the album signing, we are lined up to make our albums signed. I was just few steps away from her when I started to shake and cry. The staffs are comforting me already, asking me to breathe and if I'm okay. When I am already in front of Hailee she asked me "How are you? " but instead I answered "I love you " while crying, she was worried, she said "Don 't cry, are you okay? " then my time is up already! That is the worst and at the same time best 50 seconds of my fangirl life. I 'm lucky enough to see her close and let my album signed because I know soon she will be big and will have her own concert already.
